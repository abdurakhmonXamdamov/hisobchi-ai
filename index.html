Problem & Solution

Understanding the challenge and our innovative approach

The Problem

Finance

Banks in Uzbekistan are overwhelmed: up to 60–70% of daily customer inquiries are repetitive financial questions, creating long queues in branches, overloading call centers, and forcing front-line staff to spend hours explaining the same basic procedures. This results in delayed services, inconsistent consultations, and high employee stress, while more than 50% of citizens still lack basic financial literacy, leading to repeated visits, mistakes in documents, and growing customer frustration.

Key Issues:

  • Branches under pressure: Overcrowded, slow, resource-heavy, costly
  • The repetition trap: Same questions, wasted time, exhausted staff
  • A nation of confusion: Customers don’t understand banking, repeated visits
  • Silent burnout: Stress, overload, burnout, low service energy

Our Solution

AI-Powered Innovation

AI Accounter becomes a 24/7 personal digital banking consultant, answering questions instantly, explaining banking in simple words, automatically preparing accurate documents, and recommending the best financial solutions — reducing queues, saving staff time, preventing mistakes, lowering stress, and making banking easier for everyone.

Expected Impact:

  • 24/7 Consulting AI: Always answering, no waits, no stress → answers common questions anytime.
  • Finance Explainer AI: Simplifies banking terms, builds confidence → explains bank products in easy words.
  • Workload Relief AI: Frees staff, cuts repetition, reduces burnout → reduces pressure on employees.
  • Queue Reducing AI: Less crowd, faster guidance, happier users → lowers branch congestion.

Our Motivation

Every day in Uzbekistan, customers walk into banks confused and unsure where to start, while employees are overwhelmed repeating the same explanations again and again. Long lines, unclear steps, and low financial awareness create stress on both sides.We built Hisobchi AI because no one — neither customers nor bank staff — should feel helpless when dealing with money. Our mission is to give every citizen clear, reliable financial guidance anytime, and to let bank employees focus on real tasks instead of routine questions. Most importantly, we want to close the financial knowledge gap that limits people’s opportunities. Because financial understanding shouldn’t be a privilege — it’s a right every person deserves.
